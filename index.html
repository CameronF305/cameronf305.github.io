Who is CameronF305?

CameronF305 is an American musician, composer, producer, and content creator that is open for commissions. Cameron has worked on several professional projects as a composer, producer, production assistant, audio engineer, and much more. He has also released several projects on his own and continues to release content on YouTube to this day. Learn more on the about page.
